## Definitions

### The commutator subgroup of a group

```agda
module _
  {l : Level} (G : Group l)
  where

  subgroup-commutator-Group : Subgroup l G
  subgroup-commutator-Group =
    subgroup-family-of-elements-Group G
      ( λ x → commutator-Group G (pr1 x) (pr2 x))
```
